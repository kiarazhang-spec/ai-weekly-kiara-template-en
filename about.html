About this brief

Kiara Weekly · The AI Signal Sheet — one weekly filter through Silicon Valley's AI industry noise.
Independently written by Kiara Zhang, this brief is a weekly synthesis of the Western AI industry — primary sources first, secondary reporting only for context. The goal isn't real-time news; it's to distill each week's signals into a structured record with two layers of interpretation ("Angle" and "Take") that PMs, investors, and researchers can revisit weeks later.

🗓️ Cadence

  • Published every Monday at 8am Pacific Time, covering the previous Monday through Sunday (PT)
  • Full archive lives on the homepage, ordered by covered week

📚 Source hierarchy

  • S-tier — official company channels: OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepMind, Meta AI, Mistral, xAI, Hugging Face blogs; earnings calls, SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q, S-1); arXiv abstracts
  • A-tier — Stratechery, The Information, Latent Space, Semianalysis, Import AI, Interconnects, Platformer, FT, Bloomberg, Reuters, Wall Street Journal, TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired, MIT Technology Review
  • B-tier — auditable industry analysis with clickable primary references (used for context, never the sole citation)

🔗 Citation policy

  • Content behind an unverifiable paywall is not cited
  • Secondary reposts don't count — trace claims back to the primary source
  • Every claim carries a clickable URL to the original
  • Chinese-language reporting is out of scope for this English edition — see the Chinese sister site

⚖️ Editorial limits

  • "Angle" and "Take" callouts are inferences and interpretations of public information
  • Not investment, purchasing, or partnership advice, and not the position of any employer
  • Opinion sections are subjective by design; check the primary source and your own research

✉️ Feedback

  • Factual errors: cite the primary source and file a correction via GitHub Issues
  • Collaboration, republication, or citation requests: contact Kiara via GitHub profile
